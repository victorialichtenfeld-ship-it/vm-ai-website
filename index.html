Get Clients with Meta Ads

Run Facebook & Instagram ads that send qualified leads directly to your AI receptionist or booking page. Here's exactly how to set it up.

Step 01

Create a Meta Business Account

Go to business.facebook.com → click "Create Account." Connect your Facebook Page. This is your ad headquarters.

Step 02

Install the Meta Pixel

In Business Manager → Events Manager → Connect Data Source → Web → Pixel. Copy your Pixel ID and paste it into the YOUR_PIXEL_ID placeholder already in this site's code.

Step 03

Create a Lead Campaign

Ads Manager → Create → Campaign Objective: Leads or Conversions. Target: local area, ages 30–55, business owners. Budget: start at $20–$30/day.

Step 04

Use an Instant Form or Landing Page

Best approach: send traffic to this website's "Book a Call" section. Or use Meta's Instant Form — collect name, phone, email directly in the ad without leaving Facebook.

Step 05

Set Up a Retargeting Audience

Custom Audience → Website Visitors → Last 30 Days. Run retargeting ads to people who visited your site but didn't book. This is where your AI Follow-Up tool shines.

Step 06

Track Conversions Back to Ads

Add fbq('track', 'Lead') to your "Thank You" page or Stripe success URL so Meta knows which ads are driving actual paying customers.
